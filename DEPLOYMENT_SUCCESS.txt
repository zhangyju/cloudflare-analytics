Quick Setup (Recommended)
  $ cd ~/cloudflare-analytics
  $ export CLOUDFLARE_API_TOKEN="[your_token]"
  $ ./setup-logpush.sh

Manual Setup
  1. Visit: https://dash.cloudflare.com/?to=/:account/analytics/logpush
  2. Click: "Create Logpush Job"
  3. Dataset: HTTP Requests
  4. Frequency: Low (hourly - cost effective)
  5. Destination: R2 → cf-logs bucket
  6. Enable the job
